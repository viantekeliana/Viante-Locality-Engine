# Vianté Locality Engine

**A Cultural Intelligence Layer for AI Systems**  
_Where Culture Meets Intelligence_

Vianté Locality Engine is a communication and reasoning middleware that enables AI systems to operate **without causing dignity loss, cultural breakdown, or evaluation bias** across global contexts.

Built on the **Vianté Cultural Reasoning Framework™**, this engine applies 18 empirically derived cultural factors to transform, assess, and adapt AI communication in real time.

---

## Why This Exists

Modern AI systems fail silently across cultures.

They are:
- Evaluated through narrow Western norms
- Penalizing indirect, honor-based, or relational communication
- Misclassifying culturally correct behavior as evasion, dishonesty, or incompetence
- Creating reputational, legal, and diplomatic risk at scale

**Locality Engine exists to prevent these failures before they occur.**

## What Problem It Solves

### AI Failure Modes Addressed
- Cultural misinterpretation
- Evaluation bias in human and AI assessments
- Dignity erosion in user interactions
- Post-colonial trust breakdown
- Linguistic cognitive overload
- False negatives in performance evaluation

This is not translation.  
This is **reasoning alignment**.
